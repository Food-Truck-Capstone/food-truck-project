Personas

Food Truck Owner Persona

User Story:

As a first time user, I want to sign up for the food truck tracker application

Name:

  • John Don

Technology:

  • Devices:
    • iPhone 10 running iOS 16 and uses google app
    • Work laptop running Windows 10
  • Proficiency:
    • Not very technologically savvy but is wanting to learn more to grow his business
    • Runs small social media accounts trying to advertise his business
    • Follows the latest local food truck news to try to get a step up on the competition
    • Welcomes all new apps because he thinks it will help get the word out about his business

Attitudes & Needs

  • John wants to find easy and free ways to advertise his food truck
  • Even though John is from a different generation he welcomes all new technology even though he doesn't fully grasp it
  • John has grandkids that are often trying to teach him new things in the technological world
  • John will try all the new food trucks in town to see how he sizes up to the competition
  • John is hesitant to put personal information into new websites as he has gotten his information stolen before

Frustrations

  • Does not like when people come look at his food truck menu then don't order
  • Does not like when people can not locate his food truck as he moves around
  • John does not like when he sets up his food truck and does not get a lot of business because he feels like it is time wasted he could be spending with family.
  • John does not like confusing UIs as he is still trying to get comfortable with new technology
  • John does not like when apps say they are free then when signing up they ask for credit card information

Use Case/Interaction Flow

Pre-Condition: John has googled food truck tracker and has clicked our webpage which has brought him to the homepage

Post-Condition: John has signed up his food truck for the food truck tracker application

  • Splash screen pops up on page that asks if the user is a customer or a food truck owner
  • John clicks food truck owner
  • Webpage shows a new splash screen that asks if he is a new user or an existing user
  • John clicks new user
  • John gets redirected to the new user sign up screen
  • John enters his personal information and clicks next
  • The webpage redirects John to an intake form
  • John enters operating hours, food type, payment method, then he clicks next.
  • The webpage goes to the next screen which ask him to add menu items.
  • John clicks on the add item icon.
  • The webpage allows for a title, description, and price.
  • John enters the information for the items on his menu, then clicks next.
  • The webpage then refreshes and ask John if he agrees to report his location during his operating hours.
  • John clicks on agrees and he is directed to his vendor profile.

Food Truck Customer Persona

User Story:

As a power user I want to be able to locate my favorite food trucks quickly.

Name:

  • Xavier Martinez

Technology:

  • Device(s):
    • Latest Samsung Android
  • Proficiency:
    • Power User
  • Hates/Loves
    • Xavier Loves Food
    • Xavier is always up to try the newest food truck
    • Xavier loves sharing his latest food truck finds with his friends.
    • Xavier Hates to drive around looking for food.
    • Xavier hates it when he can't find a menu to look at for a food truck.
    • Xavier hates it when his food is cold and so he doesn't like calling in advance or ordering from delivery services; i.e. DoorDash
    • Xavier hates it when he doesn't know if a vendor only accepts cash.
    • Xavier Loves to meet up with friends at local food truck stops.
    • Xavier has favorite food dishes at different food trucks that he sometimes has trouble finding.
    • Xavier hates when he's craving a certain food but has to settle for a different dish/truck because he doesn't know where the food truck he wants food from is located.

Attitudes & Needs

  • Why Are They Using The Site?
    • Xavier would like to be able to locate nearby food trucks and see the menu in one simple application and see the payment method the vendor accepts.
  • What Need Does This Persona Have That The Site Aims To Fill?
    • The ability to quickly and efficiently locate his favorite foods/trucks based off of his desire for that day.
  • Why Choose Your Site Over Other?
    • The other platform available doesn't provide real time location data. In that application the vendor sets a schedule and location but doesn't take into account weather or a lack of customers. so if the vendor moves the customer has no way of knowing unless the vendor updates their location.

Use Case/Interaction Flow

Pre-Condition: Xavier has used the website before and is in his bookmarks and has also favorited several food trucks.

Post-Condition: Xavier locates a food truck that offers the type of food he is looking for on the app and gets directions to it.

  • Splash screen pops up on the page that asks if the user is a customer or a food truck owner.
  • Xavier clicks "customer"
  • The webpage refreshes and ask him if he would like to sign up for an account or sign in if he is already a registered member.
  • Xavier selects "sign in" and enters in his login credentials.
  • The webpage refreshes and takes him to his customer home screen.
  • On the customer's home screen Xavier can see his favorited food trucks and click to get directions to one of them.
  • Xavier scrolls down on the webpage to live map feed and clicks on the filter button below it and selects "filter by type"
  • The a popup window opens and ask Xavier to select a food type.
  • Xavier clicks on "Mexican"
  • The popup window closes and the list below the map refreshes to display only Mexican food trucks near him.
  • Xavier selects the one that is closest to him and the app launches his phones internal navigation application to take him to the food trucks location.
